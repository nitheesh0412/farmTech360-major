NUTRITION RECOMMENDATION

SUB CLINICAL MASTITIS

  1. High-Quality Forage:
    • Alfalfa hay
    • Grass hay (timothy, orchardgrass, bermudagrass)
    • Legume-grass mixtures
  2. Balanced Concentrate Feed:
    • Corn
    • Barley
    • Oats
    • Soybean meal
  3. Supplemental Feeds:
    • Brewers yeast
    • Prebiotic, Probiotic supplements
    • Omega-3 fatty acid supplements (flaxseed oil, fish oil)
  4. Hydration:
    • Ensure clean, fresh water is available at all times.
  5. Mineral and Vitamin Supplements:
    • Zinc
    • Selenium
    • Vitamin E,A,D

LUMPY SKIN

  1. Energy-Dense Feed:
    • Corn
    • Barley
    • Oats
    • Wheat
  2. Protein-Rich Diet:
    • Soybean meal
    • Fish meal
    • Cottonseed meal
  3. Vitamin Supplementation:
    • Vitamin A (beta-carotene)
    • Vitamin D,E,C
  4. Mineral Supplementation
    • Zinc
    • Copper
    • Selenium
    • Manganese
  5. Hydration and Comfort:
    • Ensure clean, fresh water is available at all times
    • Provide comfortable resting areas and shelter

NORMAL COW

  1. High-Quality Forage: Grass hay, Alfalfa hay
  2. Balanced Concentrate Feed like corn, barley, oats, wheat bran, soyabean meal
  3. Supplemental Feeds: Molasses, Cottonseed meal, Beet pulp, Brewer's yeast
  4. Mineral and Vitamin Supplements like salt, calcium, potassium, magnesium, phosphorus, Vitamin A,D,E,K
  5. Ensure clean, fresh water is available at all times
  6. Avoid Overfeeding and Obesity
  7. Regular Monitoring and Health Checks
  8. Provide clean and comfortable housing facilities
  9. Ensure adequate ventilation and temperature control
  10. Implement biosecurity measures to prevent the spread of diseases
  11. Quarantine new animals before introducing them to the herd
  12. Follow a vaccination schedule recommended by a veterinarian
  13. Implement a parasite control program
